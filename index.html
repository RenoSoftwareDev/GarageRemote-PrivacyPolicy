Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the GarageRemote app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Giangrazio (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application does not obtain any information when you download and use it. Registration is not required to use the Application.

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. The location permission is required solely to perform Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) scans necessary for the application's functionality. The Application does not store or track your location data.

Does the Application require any permissions?

To operate effectively, the Application requires the following permissions:

  • Location Permission: Required to perform Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) scans. This permission is used temporarily for scanning and is not used to collect or store precise location information.
  • Bluetooth Permission: Required to enable Bluetooth communication with BLE devices. This permission allows the Application to send and receive data to and from BLE devices.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Since the Application does not collect any information, no data is shared with third parties.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all permissions and access by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.
